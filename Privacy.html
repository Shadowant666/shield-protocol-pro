Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 22, 2026

Shield Protocol Pro ("we," "us," or "our") provides a security extension designed to preview transaction outcomes for Web3 users. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about the data we handle.

1. Information We Collect

To provide our core security services, we handle the following types of information:

  • Transaction Data: When you initiate a transaction in a web3 wallet (like MetaMask), our extension intercepts the transaction details (to, from, input data, and value). This data is sent over an encrypted (HTTPS) connection to our secure server to perform a simulation.
  • Account Information: We collect your email address when you attempt to sync a PayPal payment. This is used solely to verify your "Pro" or "Per-Use" license status via the PayPal API.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Simulation Service: Transaction data is used temporarily to generate a "Risk Report" (e.g., predicted asset changes) so you can review it before signing.
  • License Verification: Your email is used only to check for active PayPal subscriptions or token purchases.
  • No Sale of Data: We never sell your personal data, transaction history, or email address to third parties. We do not use your data for advertising or creditworthiness checks.

3. Data Storage and Security

  • Encryption: All data transmitted between the extension and our server is encrypted using industry-standard TLS/SSL (HTTPS).
  • Retention: Transaction data is processed in real-time and is not stored permanently. Email addresses are used only for authentication purposes.

4. Third-Party Services

We interact with the following third parties to provide our service:

  • Tenderly API: To simulate transactions and provide security insights.
  • PayPal: To process payments and verify licensing.

5. User Rights

You may stop all collection of information by uninstalling the extension. Since we do not maintain persistent user profiles, we do not store data that can be "corrected," but you may contact us with any privacy concerns at [Akbouret@gmail.com].
